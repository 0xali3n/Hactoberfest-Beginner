1. O2 Cafe de la Ville – A Popular Joint

One of the most popular joints, O2 Café de la Ville combines a library with a cafe so that you can enjoy a read while sipping on your favorite beverage. The stylish decor lends an upscale look to the place which is furthered by its ordering system – on iPad Minis. The cafe even has valet parking!

Must Try: Risotto, red velvet kulfi, tofu and tomato scramble

2. Smoke Café – Get The Laid-Back Vibe

Smoke Café Indore is another popular place to hang out. The laid-back, homely vibe is perfect for those looking to unwind at the end of the day. It’s also a good venue for birthday parties as it offers deals and custom decorations.

Must Try: Pasta

3. Mr. Beans – With A Crisp Decor

Clean, crisp décor is what greets you as you enter Mr. Beans. It’s a great cafe in Indore for couples with its relaxed ambiance and French and Italian menu. It’s on the pricey side but totally worth it as you’ll come to realize when you savor the delicious fare.

Must Try: Garlic bread, pizza, nachos, pasta

4. Mangosteen Café – Chill-Out

The rooftop-themed Mangosteen Café is a charming chill-out spot in New Palasia with great seating arrangements. What sets it apart, however, is the use of recycled materials in its decor. It also promotes artists by giving them a venue to showcase their work. The lively ambiance makes it an ideal place for friends and family.

Must Try: Burgers, pasta, sizzlers, coffee

5. Cafe Terazza – Rooftop Cafe

Another rooftop café with a friendly ambiance and one delicious dish after another, Café Terazza is one of the best coffee shops in Indore. It’s the perfect place for a date or an evening with friends. As dusk falls, the café is illuminated with pretty fairy lights. Like many upscale cafes, prices are on the higher end but you really get good bang for your buck.

Must Try: Pizza, nachos, lasagna

6. Café Palette – A Striking Decor

Café Palette stands out for its décor which lends plenty of character to the establishment. Splashes of color brighten the interiors, lending credence to its name. It’s located on the first floor of a three-storey building. The ground floor houses an ice cream parlor, artisan bakery, and an exhibition hall for artists while the rooftop hosts al fresco parties.

Must Try: Burgers, sandwiches, coffee

7. Cafe @ Blu – Get The Homely Ambiance

Café @ Blu is one of the coziest coffee shops in Indore and if you love a homely ambiance, this is where you should head to. It’s located in the lifestyle store, Blu, which sells stylish artifacts. The café is notable for having premium London Dairy ice creams on its menu. It also serves Greek pizzas, Belgian waffles and classic Mexican favorites like quesadillas.

Must Try: Pizza, sandwich, bruschetta, ice cream

8. Mocha – Taste Amazing Food

Mocha is one of the few establishments in Indore that combines a café and a bar. It has outlets in nine states and this is one of them. Daily specials and themed evenings ensure guests are never left bored. Live music and karaoke are also held. You should get here early as tables fill up fast. Mocha offers a very diverse menu of food, dessert and drinks.

Must Try: Sizzlers, mezze platter

9. Mclaren’s Café – Your Rustic Cafe Choice

A small, cozy joint with rustic wooden décor, Mclaren’s Café provides a quick escape for youngsters and adults to savor favorites like falafel, nachos, pizzas and pastas. Keep an eye out for the resident dog who loves to steal the attention of guests!

Must Try: Garlic bread, nachos, pasta

10. Oakaz Cafe & Restaurant – With A Wide Menu

Oakaz Café & Restaurant is a lovely hangout place with a rooftop seating option. The eclectic décor will have you gazing around in curiosity and admiration! The menu is a mix of Indian, continental, Chinese and regular café fare like sandwiches.

Must Try: Pizza, burger, crispy corn

11. Mama Loca – For Authentic Flavors

Would you like to taste authentic continental food and want to transport into a different world? Then visit the Moca Loca Cafe that has an amazing European ambiance which reminds you of tudor houses. Apart from the innovation in their menu, the cafe has also worked to make its live music and karaoke section interesting. This is your ideal weekend outing!

Must Try: Pita e Hummus and Pesto, Hot basil chicken salad, Nachos

12. Hobnob Gourmet Cafébar – Experience Life Full Of Joy

Established at one of the most lively places to enjoy nightlife in Indore, the cafe has a vibrant environment. Expect some awesome live music in the evenings and enjoy a barbecue party on every Sunday. Weekends or weekdays, now say goodbye to monotony and all the dull moments of your life.

Must Try: Thin Crust Pizza, Pasta

13. Mitti Cafè – Taste Organic Choices

Inspired by the local and original sources of food, Mitti serves fresh, organic and desi flavors. This is one of the best cafes In Indore serving organic produce. Dealing in all sorts of desi flavors, expect to taste awesome thallis, adrak wali chai and mathri. The food is collected from a handpicked set of farmers and certified vendors who provide cold pressed oil and other ingredients. Also enjoy the several board games.

Must Try: Pav Bhaji, Crispy Corn, Chole Kulche

14. Eyry Cafe – With A Casual Ambiance

A soothing ambiance, classic Indian delicacies and a perfectly casual atmosphere makes Eyry one of the top choices for office-goers. While the wide range of beverages like iced teas, hot coffees and frappes will impress you, the food laced with quick bites and snacks will grapple you. You can choose the indoor or outdoor seating options!

Must Try: Cheese Balls, Paneer Tikka

15. Cafe Chapter One – Start One Today

Visit Cafe Chapter One if you are looking to start the next chapter of your life. A sleek and modern outpost serving drinks, espressos and meals will be a cozy destination for your date. Witness the guaranteed pleasure to taste the best of both beverages – coffee and tea. You can choose to eat some amazing continental dishes with lip-smacking siders.

Must Try: Coffee, Tea, Mini Burger Platter, Sandwich, Cappuccino

16. Tea Diaries Cafe – A Cozy Destination

The Tea Diaries Cafe is another cozy and chill destination to enjoy food and have little talks at any time of the week. With indoor as well as outdoor seating, the cafe has been well decorated while keeping a modern outlook in mind. This is also one of those few Indore cafes serving meals that are affordable, especially for students. A plan here is only going to strengthen your friendship!

Must Try: Tea, Pav Bhaji, Green Apple Mojito

17.Nirvana Cafe – Attain Some Solace

Are you looking for solace and peace? Then spend a good 3 hours at this cozy little cafe. You get great food, books, board games, soulful music, guitar and lastly the priceless moments of leisure. You can visit here any time of the week – even twice a week and still spend significantly less, provided you come alone.

Must Try: Hakka Noodles, Brownie

18.Lotus Hut Cafe – The Best For Students

Quick bites, fast food, soothing drinks – do you need anything else? What about money? We say under 200, Indian rupees. Yes, it’s that affordable and the greatness doesn’t end here. The freshly baked and mouth-watering products coming fresh out of the oven will keep your mouth full. For the ultimate refreshments, visit Lotus Hut Cafe. This is one of the best cafes in Indore for students.

Must Try: Coffee, Tea, Sandwiches
